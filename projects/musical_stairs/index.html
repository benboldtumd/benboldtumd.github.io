Musical Stairs Sensor



Apparently people ask why The Works doesn't have musical stairs. THAT CAN NOT GO ON FOREVER, I'm determined. Because I too have come to expect sound effects when I go up and down stairs.

It's not something that's actually being planned or anything, but I thought I'd work on a sensor. The ones we're used to have a single infrared tripwire. I am working on a design that uses two red (visible) tripwires and the time period between the tripping of the two should be an indication of how much of a stomp has occurred. This information could translate into sound velocity, or maybe harmonics or some other nifty thing that would make ours stand out from other musical stairs just a little bit.

This is more of an R & D project. Right now I need to focus on the sensor curtain and other projects.
