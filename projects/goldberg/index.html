Rube Goldberg Machine
For PHYS 2012

This is an extra credit project that I worked on for one of my general physics classes. It was a minimal amount of extra credit, but when I saw an opportunity to combine a 1993 Macintosh, a fast 68HC12 microcontroller and some toys from when I was little in such a way that it would come to life (!), I just poured all sorts of creativity into it.

This page is an overview of how the machine worked, from start to finish. The Mac was used to project the 16-bit 640x480 images onto a large screen in the lecture hall.
