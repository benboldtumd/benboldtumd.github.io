2-Phase Stepper Motor Driver


I have lots of motors, and lots of stepper motors. Most are the more simple 6-wire variety, that require you to step the wires in a simple sequence. But I have this strange little stepper motor that only has 3 wires. One wire is common, and the other two are alternating inputs. The current must switch direction as part of the sequence. With respect to common, the sequence is as follows:


StepInput 1Input 2
1+24 Volts0 Volts
20 Volts+24 Volts
3-24 Volts0 Volts
40 Volts-24 Volts
