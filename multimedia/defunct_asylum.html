THE DEFUNCT ASYLUM
Synthesizer: Apple Logic
Date: April 30, 2011
Type: Remix
Transcription: Direct Modification 		From Brain Damage, Pink Floyd. Exploring the grounds and traversing the tunnel system of the defunct Willmar State Hospital reveals an erie heaviness whose individualistic, troubled existence is more than unmistakable. Maybe not immediately apparent are the true intentions of this facility and its patrons, past and present.
